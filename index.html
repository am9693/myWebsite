Attractions

There are many wonderful attractions in Singapore, ranging from themeparks, to aquariams and to zoos! Below, you will find my favourite attractions in Singapore!

  • Sentosa!

    Sentosa is one of the best places to have fun with your friends and family at their variety of themeparks and activities!

  • Singapore Zoo


    Singapore Zoo is a fabulous place to hang out with your loved ones whilst enjoying the range of activities they have to offer! With a wide variety of restaurants to serve your taste-buds and so many safaris and animals, you will definitely find something to do!

  • Gardens by the Bay!


    Gardens by the Bay is a breathtaking garden in Singapore, showcasing the famous Supertree Grove along with two impressive attractions – the Flower Dome and Cloud Forest. Situated by the Marina Bay waterfront, it beautifully combines nature with modern design, providing visitors with a distinctive green retreat right in the city's center.

Food

The food in Singapore is simply magnificent! With their hawker stalls and fine-dining options, you will never be hungry! Here are my favourite breakfast, lunch and dinner places to go:


  • Breakfast: Everton Food Place!


    I LOVE Everton Food Place! With their wide variety of food options, my personal favourite is their prata with egg and iced milo!

  • Lunch: SUN with MOON Japanese Dining & Cafe


    I love going to Sun with Moon on Orchard Road with my family. The sushi, sashimi, and teppanyaki are always fresh, and the atmosphere is relaxed and friendly. It's one of our favorite spots to enjoy a meal together.

  • Dinner: Hawker Stalls!


    Hawker centres in Singapore are the perfect spot for a casual dinner, offering affordable and delicious local dishes like chicken rice, laksa, and satay. It’s a great place to enjoy a relaxed evening with a wide variety of flavors to choose from, all in one place.
